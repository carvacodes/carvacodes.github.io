Rachel Turgoose

Ground-up site build, brand development

Complete site overhaul, with logo design and light brand development

This build was designed with an eye for the client's upcoming ventures as a professor and PhD candidate. The font choices, color scheme, site copy, and general design convey professionalism and integrity, essential to the client's current and future station in the field of academia. The client's work as a music professional is subtly made to run parallel to this, with an intuitive media page and a performance calendar featuring early.

  • Highlights:
  • Concise, bold, and professional site copy
  • Simple mailto-driven contact form with light client-side validation
  • Significant photo-touchups and web-optimization
  • Visit rachelturgoose.com

AJC Jazz

Ground-up site build

Multi-functional website to serve as a portal for clients seeking to hire live entertainment, obtain music lessons, or find info on upcoming performances.

My own entertainment company website, this build makes use of large splash images with strong depth-of-field effects, along with concise section descriptions and easy-to-locate buttons, to quickly call the user to action. A simple PHP/JSON-driven calendar management system, behind an authentication wall, was also implemented.

  • Highlights:
  • Detailed FAQ sections for entertaiment booking and lessons
  • Simple, easy-to-read calendar
  • Calendar management system in PHP/JSON with authentication
  • Visit ajcjazz.com

Glass Elephant Pro

Ground-up build, brand development

Worked directly with Glass Elephant's owner developing a fully-functional site from wireframes under a tight deadline. Wrote a vast majority of copy for the site, and optimized images considerably.

Making the site user-friendly for a wide demographic was of particular importance to Glass Elephant. Several design elements and branding options were tested with the client before the current site iteration was finalized.

  • Highlights:
  • Complete brand overhaul; heavy image authoring and editing work
  • Crafted fast, lightweight JavaScript solution for FAQ and Equipment page navigation
  • Assisted with site migration and hosting solutions
  • Visit glasselephantpro.com

about Arielle

I'm a web developer currently living in Austin, designing websites and building customer-facing web solutions for clients on a freelance basis. I also occasionally work as a web design consultant.

I'm a fanatic about code and design--one of those that can stay up through the wee hours getting things pixel-perfect.

When I'm not digging through code, you're likely to find me either behind a game controller or at a jazz club in Austin with a guitar.
